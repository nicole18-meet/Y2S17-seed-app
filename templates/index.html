DISEASES


Welcome to Diseases! Our platform allows you to learn and discover more about different diseases and/or medical conditions out there.
In addition to the wide knowledge found here, you get an exclusive expirience allowing you to go through the daily steps a real patient would have to go through.
Another special feature is stories written by the patients themselves, showing the real point of view we are trying to put out there.

We set ourselves a goal to help raise awareness about medical conditions and the consequences patients have to deal with as their result.

If you are dealing with a medical condition and would like to share your story, we invite you to send it to us to our email.
The stories will be checked and verified before they get uploaded.
