HOW TO HELP A LOVED ONE COPE WITH DIABETES?
*When people have the support of their family and friends, they are able to better manage their diabetes. It is a hard disease to handle alone. You can help your loved one cope with diabetes by showing your support. These tips tell you how. 1- Walking together daily gives you time to talk and stay active.
2- Find ways to help your loved one manage the stress of living with diabetes. Being a good listener is often the most important thing you can do to help.
3- Ask your loved one if he or she would like reminders about doctor visits, when to check blood sugar, and when to take medicine.
4- Help your loved one write a list of questions for the health care team.
5- Eat well. Help your loved one make meals that include foods such as fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
6- Find things you can do together such as walking, dancing, or gardening. Being active is a great way to handle stress.
7- Walking together daily gives you time to talk and stay active.
8- Cut back on sweets by serving fresh fruit for dessert.

YOU CAN ALSO HELP THESE PEOPLE BY DONATING AND/OR BY GIVING THEM MOTIVATION BY SHARING YOUR PERSONAL STORIES OR WRITING INSPIRATIONAL POSITIVE ENERGY GIVING QUOTES/THOUGHTS.
