HOW CAN I HELP?


HOW TO HELP A LOVED ONE COPE WITH MEDICAL CONDITIONS?


When people have the support of their family and friends, they are able to manage their diseases better.
More often than not, these situations are hard to handle alone, therefore you can help your loved one cope with them by showing your support.
These tips will tell you how.

  • Walking together daily gives you time to talk and stay active.
  • Find ways to help your loved one manage the stress of living with their condition. Being a good listener is often the most important thing you can do to help.
  • Ask your loved one if he or she would like reminders about doctor visits, when to check their general state, and when to take medicine.
  • Help your loved one write a list of questions for the health care team.
  • Eat well. Help your loved one make meals that include foods such as fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
  • Find things you can do together such as walking, dancing, or gardening. Being active is a great way to handle stress.
  • Cut back on sweets by serving fresh fruit for dessert.

Help can also be given by donating and/or giving motivation by sharing your own personal stories.
